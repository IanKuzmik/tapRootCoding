Diagnostics and Custom-Built Websites

There are many reasons why you might want a website, be it for your growing small business or just a hobby blog. Whatever the purpose, there are a few principles that every website should strive to abide by.

Smooth Performance

Page load times are one of the biggest factors affecting your website's retention rate. And whether that means a loss in revenue, drop in followers, or simply your friends losing interest, no one likes a slow website.

I Can Help:

  • Optimize Images
  • Minify Stylesheets
  • Defer External Scripts
  • Implement Lazy Loading

I'll Focus On:

  • Authentication
  • SSL Certificates
  • Proper HTTP Headers
  • Preventing SQL Injection

Thorough Security

Not only can poor security practices affect your data, it can affect your visibility as well. With tech giants like Google constantly raising the bar and pushing us towards a safer, more consistent online experience, don't see your website left behind due to poor security maintenaince or lack of security features to begin with!

Sustainability

Yes, even websites have a carbon footprint. The transfer and processing of data over the internet has a massive impact on our carbon emissions and ultimately the stability of our climate. And, especially when dealing with larger projects, tortuous code maintenaince and poor SEO can leak valuble time and energy for you and the user.
